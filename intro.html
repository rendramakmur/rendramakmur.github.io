Read How to Play & Rules below.

How to Play & the Rules:

  1. Find someone to play with, could be your family, friend, or your significant other.
  2. It's okay if you don't have one. You could ask yourself anyway.
  3. Click Let's Outspoken button.
  4. Shuffle the outspokenspot cards by click the Pick Random Card button.
  5. Once it stops, answer the questions on the card.
  6. Make sure you answer the question outspokenly.
  7. You could do this over and over and you won't get same question at one go.
